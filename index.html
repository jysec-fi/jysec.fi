WHAT IS JYSEC?

JySec gathers professionals, students and hobbyists that are interested in Information and Cyber Security in Central Finland. The purpose of JySec is to organise meetups and get-togethers to share, learn, connect in the field of Cybersecurity.

JySec is part of CitySec
